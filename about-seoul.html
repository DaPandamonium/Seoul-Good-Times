Why "Seoul Good Times"?

Our restaurant's name, "Seoul Good Times," reflects our commitment to bring the joy and soul of Korean cuisine into every dish we serve. Each recipe tells a story of traditional flavors meeting modern creativity. Join us on a culinary journey through the history of Seoul's street food markets to today's innovative Korean dining experiences.

Menu Highlights

Kimchi Jjigae

The heartwarming Kimchi Jjigae, a staple in Korean cuisine, features a rich broth made from ripe kimchi, providing a balance of spicy, sour, and umami flavors. It's perfect for any meal, offering a glimpse into the soul of Korean home cooking.

Bulgogi

Bulgogi, meaning "fire meat," is made with thin slices of beef marinated in a sweet and savory sauce. Grilled to perfection, it offers a melt-in-your-mouth texture that's beloved by food enthusiasts worldwide.

Bibimbap

Bibimbap, translating to "mixed rice," is a vibrant bowl of warm white rice topped with seasoned vegetables, chili pepper paste, soy sauce, a fried egg, and sliced meat. Enjoy the harmony of colors and flavors as you mix them together.
